Problem

Hiring is overloaded while mobile applicants are underserved.

Job seekers increasingly rely on mobile devices, yet many job boards and career portals are not optimized for true mobile application experiences. At the same time, applications per job have surged while open job postings decline.

Job seekers

  • Desktop-centric hiring ecosystems are poorly optimized for mobile use.
  • Lengthy applications and repetitive data entry reduce completion rates.
  • Opaque AI screening processes contribute to user frustration.

Employers

  • Overwhelming applicant volume and limited recruiter bandwidth.
  • AI-assisted keyword filtering fails to surface qualified candidates.
  • Bias concerns with AI-driven screening tools.

Customer Analysis

HireOn serves employers and job seekers, with employers as primary revenue customers.

Employers

Small to mid-sized businesses, campus recruiters, and early-career hiring teams face:

  • Overwhelming applicant volume.
  • Inefficient resume screening and recruiter time loss.
  • Applicant tracking systems reliant on keyword density.

Job seekers

Students and recent graduates experience:

  • Fragmented, desktop-centric application workflows.
  • Low mobile completion rates.
  • Frustration from opaque AI screening.

Solution

HireOn reduces application friction while enabling intentional, role-based screening.

HireOn is a B2B, multi-sided SaaS platform that serves employers and job seekers. It enables candidates to apply seamlessly from mobile devices and empowers employers to screen applicants through predefined, qualification-based roles rather than keyword density.

Mobile-first design

Built for smartphone use rather than adapted from desktop workflows.

Role-based screening

Applicants are screened through structured qualification roles.

Controlled applicant volume

Employers define how many applicants they review.

Improved hiring signal

Reduced false negatives and bias introduced by keyword-based AI filtering.

Employer workflow

  • Define qualification-based roles before screening begins.
  • Screen applicants by role-based profiles rather than keywords.
  • Specify the number of applicants to review.
  • Reduce applicant overload and improve signal quality.

Job seeker workflow

  • Apply seamlessly from mobile devices.
  • Use structured, profile-based applications.
  • Experience reduced application friction on mobile.
  • Engage with a mobile-first hiring platform.

HireOn initially targets U.S. early-career and entry-level hiring, including campus recruiting teams, internships, and junior professional roles. The service obtainable market focuses on small to mid-sized employers and campus recruiting teams, with expansion opportunities into broader enterprise hiring and specialized workforce verticals.

Competitive Differentiation

Focused, mobile-first design differentiates HireOn.

HireOn competes with LinkedIn, Indeed, and Handshake while offering a mobile-first alternative built for intentional, role-based screening.
